Lambasingi – The Kashmir of Andhra Pradesh

Lambasingi, or Lammasingi, is a serene, high-altitude village in Andhra Pradesh's Eastern Ghats, famously known as the "Kashmir of Andhra Pradesh". Located at 1,000 meters, it is South India's only location with sub-zero winter temperatures, occasional snowfall, and constant mist. It offers scenic coffee/pepper plantations and misty, hilly landscapes.
